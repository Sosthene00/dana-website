Dana Wallet

Send bitcoin to the people you love, anytime, anywhere

  • Bitcoin is your money: send and receive as you like, don't ask permission
  • Post once, receive forever: addresses can be reused without ruining your privacy
  • Organize your money into accounts, your contacts as an address book

How does it work?

  • Silent Payment is a new protocol for payment codes that make address reuse impossible and improve bitcoin user experience
  • Powered by privacy preserving Blindbit oracle backend: use a public instance or bootstrap your own

Wanna try?

Dana is still under development and at an early stage. Available package on fdroid is using a test network, not real bitcoin. Any feedback or contribution is warmly welcomed. You can build it for yourself if you know what you are doing and want to try mainnet
